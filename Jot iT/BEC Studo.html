BEC Studo

Project Details

An Android Application designed for the students of Bapatla Engineering College, which serves the needs of students such as Food and Accomodation, study materials, Resource persons(Techies), services, Placement Guidance and Materials, etc..,

Resources & Tools

  • Android Studio (IDE)
  • Hosted and deployed in Play store

Technologies

  • Firebase

Languages

  • HTML,CSS,JS (for admin to update database and give updates through simple webpages)
  • Kotlin (App dev backend)
  • XML (Designing)
